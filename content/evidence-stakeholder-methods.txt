## Stakeholder Map Template

### Classification Framework

**Primary Stakeholders:** Directly affected by the problem and solution; have direct stake in outcomes
**Secondary Stakeholders:** Indirectly affected; have interest but less direct impact on/from the issue

**Internal Stakeholders:** Within the trucking organization(s)
**External Stakeholders:** Outside the organization but connected to it

---

### Internal Stakeholders

#### **PRIMARY Internal Stakeholders**

**1. Long-Haul Truck Drivers (Employees)**
- **Role/Description:** Front-line employees experiencing turnover problem
- **Interest in Problem:** How turnover and retention affect them
- **Interest in Solution:** How proposed solutions affect their work/compensation
- **Influence Level:** Low
- **Support Level:** Supporter
- **Key Concerns:** 
    - Personal health and wellbeing
    - Fair pay
    - Fair treatment
- **Engagement Priority:** High
- **Evidence Source:** Aryal et al. (2023) semi-structured interviews

**2. Transportation Company Managers/Fleet Managers**
- **Role/Description:** Decision-makers who can directly affect driver retention
- **Interest in Problem:** Impact of turnover on operations, costs
- **Interest in Solution:** Feasibility, ROI, possible implementation challenges
- **Influence Level:** High
- **Support Level:** Supporter (if retention increases)
- **Key Concerns:** 
    - Budget 
    - Operational disruption 
    - Effectiveness 
    - ROI
- **Engagement Priority:** HIGH
- **Evidence Source:** Glodziak (2022) practitioner article

---

### External Stakeholders

#### **PRIMARY External Stakeholders**

**3. Partnered Shipping Companies**
- **Role/Description:** Companies that hire trucking services to move freight
- **Interest in Problem:** Service reliability affected by driver shortages; delivery delays
- **Interest in Solution:** Improved service consistency; but may resist cost increases
- **Influence Level:** Medium
- **Support Level:** Supporter
- **Key Concerns:** 
    - On-time delivery 
    - Service quality 
    - Reliability
- **Evidence Source:** Inbound Logistics (2025) shipper survey
