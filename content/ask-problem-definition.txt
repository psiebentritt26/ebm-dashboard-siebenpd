<h1 style="text-align: center; font-size: 20pt; font-weight: bold;">Clear Problem Statement</h1>

<h1 style="text-align: center; font-size: 20pt; font-weight: bold;">Why This Problem Matters</h1>
<h3>Impact on Organization</h3>
<p>Less goods being transported can mean decreased productivity, poor public relations due to delayed shipments, and therefore less overall profits.</p>

<h3>Stakeholder Impact</h3>
<p>Shipments to customers may be delayed; senior and middle management will have to navigate constant staff shortages and put unnecessary strains on resources.</p>
